This week I attended the NLP Summit Healthcare 2023, a free virual event organized by the John Snow Labs. It was a great event with a lot of interesting talks. I’ll share some of the key takeaways.

1. Best practices when developing NLP models

Presented in the opening keynote by Dr. David Gondek, Chief Data Scientist at John Snow Labs, he sumarized some impoeratn best practices when developing NLP models:

  1. Test your models! - why would you expect untested software to work?

  2. Don’t reuse academic models in production - publish reserach ≠ building reliable systems

  3. Test beyond accuracy - Rubustness, Bias, Fairness, Toxicity, Efficiency, Safety

3. Some organizations that are doing interesting work in NLP in healthcare:
